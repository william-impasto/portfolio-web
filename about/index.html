Design Philosophy

Or how design should be

Simple & Authentic

Just like a dough, an excellent design does not need frills and exaggerations to be considered authentic.

Adaptable & structured

A dough is an elastic structure adaptable to any container and, depending on the type, eventuality: an excellent design does this, metaphorically.

Functional & Emotional

Excellent design must not only fulfill its function: it must also be able to excite in order to create memorable experiences.
